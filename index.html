About Fly Sky Tourism & Transport

International travel expertise with hands-on destination management

Fly Sky Tourism & Transport is an international travel company with operations and partnerships across the United Arab Emirates and Georgia, providing comprehensive travel solutions for leisure travelers, corporate clients, travel agents, and tour operators worldwide.

With over 12 years of professional experience in the travel and tourism industry, our team specializes in destination management, group travel, FIT holidays, MICE events, transportation services, and tailor-made travel experiences across multiple destinations.

Over the years, we have successfully managed and coordinated travel arrangements for thousands of passengers, ranging from small private groups of 15 guests to large-scale operations exceeding 1,500 passengers.
